Bubb


A lightweight chatting app


Integerty - Simplicity - Privacy

This is what Bubb is made for.



Your conversations (or bubbles) are contained on your device only*, effectively keeping it P2P.



- Create new accounts at any time, and delete accounts at any time.

- Bubb uses a unique, passwordless system where an account is as easy as entering a Username.

- Bubbles are unique, and can be transferred using a transfer key.

- People can join and leave bubbles as they please, but new people won't see history without a transfer key.
