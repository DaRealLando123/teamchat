Bubb


A lightweight chatting app



Integrity - Simplicity - Privacy

This is what Bubb is made for.



Your conversations (or bubbles) are encrypted and contained on your device only*, effectively keeping it P2P*.


Manage accounts easily ✌🏻 Create new accounts at any time, delete old accounts at any time, transfer accounts at any time.
Minimal data collection 🗂️ All you need is an email and password, with minimal data collection.
Secure chatting 🔐 Bubb uses a unique system where bubbles can only be joined using a special key.
